About

The "Online Food Order Analysis" project aims to analyze data from an online food ordering platform to gain insights into customer preferences, ordering trends, and business performance.

Project Photos

Description

This project involves collecting and analyzing data from online food orders placed through a web or mobile application. By examining various aspects such as popular cuisines, peak ordering hours, average order value, customer ratings, and delivery times, the goal is to provide valuable insights to restaurants and food delivery services.

The analysis can help businesses optimize their menu offerings, pricing strategies, and delivery logistics to enhance customer satisfaction and increase revenue. It can also aid in identifying emerging food trends and predicting future demand.
